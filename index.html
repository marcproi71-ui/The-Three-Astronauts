Who We Are

Three friends.
One dream.
Infinite orbit.

There isn't much to say yet. In 2025, three long-time friends decided to merge their musical passion into an artistic project with a vintage flavour.

They began writing and demoing their own songs almost immediately, dedicating all of 2025 to the production of their first musical dream: Dreaming The Drum Light.

Still waiting to see their debut work published, The Three Astronauts are already working on a second album — set for release in 2027.
